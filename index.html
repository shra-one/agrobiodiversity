THE FOUNDATION OF CONSORTIUM NEPAL IS PRIMARILY BASED ON CHILDREN EDUCATION AND DEVELOPMENT

Consortium Nepal is a non-profit that works to improve the quality of education in Nepal through research, advocacy, and capacity building. The organization was founded in 1995, and works to ensure that all children in Nepal have access to quality education. They strive to create an equitable education system that is accessible to all, regardless of gender, ethnicity, or socioeconomic background. Consortium Nepal works in partnership with government and non-governmental organizations to facilitate the implementation of educational reform and development initiatives. They have successfully implemented a number of projects, such as the development of a national curriculum, quality teacher training, and the establishment of a quality assurance system. Consortium Nepal is committed to making sure that all children in Nepal have access to quality education.
